Work Experience Rules(MUST):

• Avoid generic adjectives; show impact through measurable contributions (e.g., “Led cross-functional team delivering HIPAA-compliant platform 20% faster” instead of “Strong leadership skills”).
• Align soft skills with senior-level responsibilities such as architectural decision-making, mentorship, and cross-department coordination.

Skills Section Rules:

Formatting:
